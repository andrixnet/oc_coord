coord.eu - Waypoint redirection for Opencaching sites

Waypoint redirection for opencaching sites and other geocaching resources

Supported opencaching sites:

  • OP - Poland: opencaching.pl
  • OB - BeNeLux: www.opencaching.nl
  • OR - Romania: www.opencaching.ro
  • OC - Germany: www.opencaching.de also France, Italy, Spain
  • OK - United Kingdom: www.opencache.uk
  • OU - U.S.A.: www.opencaching.us also Canada, Mexico
  • OZ - Czech Republic: www.opencaching.cz

Usage:

Waypoint:
http://coord.eu/OX1234 - OX1234 is the waypoint code.
(OX is the cache prefix of the opencaching site, see above)
Users:
http://coord.eu/OX/U123 - 123 is the user ID.
(OX is the cache prefix of the opencaching site, see above)

Supported trackables:

  • GK - GeoKrety trackables; see : geokrety.org

Usage:

GeoKret:
http://coord.eu/GK1234 - GK1234 is the GeoKret ID.
Log a GeoKret:
http://coord.eu/GK/CODE12 - CODE12 is the 6 character tracking code.
